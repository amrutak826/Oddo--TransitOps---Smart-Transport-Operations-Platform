TransitOps

Smart Transport Operations Platform

TransitOps unifies fleet, driver, dispatch, maintenance and expense operations into a single, modern control center built natively on Odoo 18. Designed for transport & logistics teams that need real-time visibility and role-based control over every vehicle, driver and trip.

Highlights

  • Operations Dashboard — live fleet health, active trips and alert feed
  • Vehicle Registry — documents, insurance/RC/permit expiry tracking
  • Driver Management — license validity, assignment history, performance
  • Trip Dispatch — scheduling, live status, automatic vehicle/driver allocation
  • Maintenance — preventive & breakdown service tracking with full cost history
  • Fuel & Expense — mileage analytics and cost-center reporting
  • Reports — operational and financial PDF/XLSX reports
  • Notifications — proactive alerts for expiries, maintenance and trip events
  • Role-Based Security — Manager, Dispatcher, Mechanic and Driver roles out of the box
